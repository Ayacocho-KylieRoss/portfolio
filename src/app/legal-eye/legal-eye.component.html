LegalEye Redesigned Icons

Objective:

Develop a refreshed set of navigation icons for LegalEye, focusing on a friendlier, more rounded visual style to enhance usability and better reflect the platform’s approachable, welcoming identity.

Design Approach:

  • Simplicity & Clarity – Ensure recognizability even at smaller sizes, minimizing visual noise.
  • Consistent & Precise Execution – Applied uniform stroke weight, designed on a 24x24 grid, and aligned icons for a cohesive and pixel-perfect interface.
  • Recognizable & Contextual – Ensured each icon is easy to identify at a glance and clearly represents its associated section or function.
  • Minimal Changes from Original – Preserved key visual elements of the original icons to maintain familiarity and reduce user relearning.

Role:

Marketing and Creatives (Intern)

Tools:
