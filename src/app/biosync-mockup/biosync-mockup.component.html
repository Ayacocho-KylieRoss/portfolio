BioSync: Seamless Attendance Tracking and Scheduling with Biometrics

Description:

BioSync is a digital system for Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Taguig Campus that automates scheduling and attendance using biometric verification. It enhances efficiency, security, and accessibility, replacing outdated manual processes with a real-time, user-friendly solution.

Role:

UI/UX Designer, Frontend Developer

Tools for Mockup:

Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Canva
